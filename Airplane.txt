stage
prompt the user to enter the plane's take off speed in m/s
collect the the plane's take off speed in m/s
store the plane's take off speed as speed

prompt the user to enter the plane's acceleration
collect the plane's acceleration
store the plane's acceleration as acceleration

calculate the minimum run way length by:
	(speed raised to the power of 2) / (2 * acceleration)

store the result as length
display length
